Project #2: DIY Onewheel


Although the DIY OneWheel, was a short lived project, It is still one of my favorites. Around the start of quarentine I found myself with a lot of freetime, and
I had been going on frequent walks. On one of these walks I saw somone riding a hoverboard, and that remineded me of an old project I had seen
online. By utilizing the the motors, gyroscope, battery, and motherboard from the hoverboard, you could create a one-wheeled self-balencing
SkateBoard, or commercially known as the OneWheel. After rembering the project the flame was reignited, and I immedatly began collecting
parts, and planning the build.
